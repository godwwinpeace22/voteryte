Frequently Asked Questions

Who can create elections?

Elections should be created by people who have the right to do so, in an orgarnization or school, e.g the faculty president

How long does it really take to create an election?

You can create an election in Less than 5 minutes

Can I create more than one election?

Yes. Fair usage policy applies

Do you offer flat rate pricing for schools?

Yes. Please contact us at hello@voteryte.com for details about our flat rate or volume pricing.

What happens if I exceed the voter size limit of my plan?

Once you exceed the limit, voters won't be able to enroll in the election. You will need to purchase a higher quota.

How do you prevent multiple voting?

We ensure voters can't vote multiple times by verifying all voters before enrollement. Voters are verified using thier BVN (Bank Verification Number). We don't store users' BVNs. Its only for verification purposes.

How are results calculated?

A contestant with the highest number of votes for each role/position is considered as the winner. When a user votes for a contestant, that counts as 1 vote.

How do I add contestants?

Contestants are to apply by themselves. The admin controls who can contest by providing access tokens to contestants that have met all his requirements. These tokens are found in the management dashboard.

How many contestants are allowed per election?

Any number of contestants can contest

When does voting start or end?

Voting starts, and stops automatically at the time specified by the election admin during the creation

How can I create a forum?

All elections come with a forum. No need to create one

What happens after my free trial expires?

You will be downgraded to the free plan. Upgrade your account to enjoy all the benefits of a Premium plan.

How do I report issues and get support?

We are here to help. Feel free to contact us if you face any challenges or need help.

Can I print election results?

Yes. Just click on the 'Results Summary' on the election dashboard, then click on the 'print' icon
