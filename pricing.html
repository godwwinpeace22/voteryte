Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer flat rate pricing for schools?

Yes. Please contact us at hello@facevote.io for details about our flat rate or volume pricing.

How long do trials last?

Every account comes with a 14 days free trial. We will notify users in advance of when the trial is coming to an end.

When can i cancel my plan?

You can cancel at any time.

What happens if I exceed the voter size limit of my plan?

Once you exceed the limit, voters won't be able to enroll in the election. You will need to purchase a higher quota.

What happens after my free trial expires?

You will be downgraded to the free plan. Upgrade your account to enjoy all the benefits of a Premium plan.

How do I report issues and get support?

We are here to help. Feel free to contact us if you face any challenges or need help.
